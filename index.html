About Us

Who We Are?

Metalbook is a full-stack digital supply-chain platform that connects a global network of metal suppliers & consumers, and offers the best prices. It also provided fabrication and customization at the best prices in the market for metal related finished and semi finished goods. By connecting offline players, we are dedicated to building a universal marketplace that believes in quick and transparent transactions along with hassle free logistics enabling end-to-end procurement solutions for the customer.

What We Think?

One of the main goals of Metalbook is to become the metals community's one-stop shop and pricing information platform, as well as to offer state-of-the-art logistical solutions for effective procurement. Our mission is to set the bar for professionalism in the services we offer, while also providing the market with excellent value and a wonderful, trustworthy experience for each and every one of our clients..

What We Do?

Metalbook facilitates transactions, enables smart matchmaking, removes manual and tedious tasks with automation and saves time by making the whole process easy and accessible. It fulfills customers’ needs with on time delivery of raw materials, traceability, and competitive pricing while introducing customization of steel products as per their needs to the current traditional market.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Metalbook making the Iron and Steel supply chain better and efficient through the use of technology?

Metalbook has developed a robust SAaS platform for the industry which provides the ability for both the buyer and seller to participate in our ecosystem and get the best deals for both the parties.

What’s the best time to contact Metalbook?

We are open to serve you 7 days a week, please feel free to contact us if you have a query or have any requirements.

What’s Metalbook’s return and refund policy?

We take full responsibility if there’s some quality difference or SKU mismatch in the order, and shall return and arrange for the replacement or refund your amount.

What items do you cater to?

We service all the Iron and steel requirements PAN India, with items available in all the leading primary and secondary manufacturers.

I'm a machinery and boiler manufacturer. I want to order Plates and MS Angles to my factory in Ludhiana, how can i do that through Metalbook?

Just sign up on the Metalbook portal, go to the 'Create Enquiry' page and Select the items you want, their make and weight, and select your delivery location. We will get in touch with you to verify your enquiry and fulfill it in the agreed time.

How does Metalbook ensures the weight and quality of my order?

Every order goes through various quality checks and thorough examination before it is loaded and dispatched to you. Metalbook gives you the quality guarantee by ensuring it at the supply end.

How can I order items through Metalbook?

Just go to our website, sign up with Metalbook and start creating your order. You can also schedule a demo with us to know more features.

How does Metalbook ensure I get the lowest rates for my requirements?

Metalbook has an extensive network and relationships with all the major manufacturers and suppliers over the country to service your requirements at the best possible rates by comparing multiple quotes, and ensuring quality in the process.

Can Metalbook also help with the credit for buying Iron and Steel items?
